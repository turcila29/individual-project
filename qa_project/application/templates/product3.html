Acqua di Gio

Acqua di Gio by Armani is a Woody Floral Musk fragrance for men. Egoiste Platinum was launched in 1993. The nose behind this fragrance is Jacques Polge. Top notes are Lavender, Rosemary, Neroli and Petitgrain; middle notes are Geranium, Clary Sage, Galbanum and Jasmine; base notes are Oakmoss, Vetiver, Cedar, Sandalwood and Amber.
