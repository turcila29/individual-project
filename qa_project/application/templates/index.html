Some personal favourites

In this section i just want to present some some of my personal favourites based on the quality for price and development process.


Chanel Platinum Egoiste EDT

Egoiste Platinum by Chanel is a Woody Floral Musk fragrance for men. Egoiste Platinum was launched in 1993


Amazing choice for a summer scent and a daily use but lacks the strenght to resist during cold weathers.

Acqua Di Gio Giorgio Armani

Acqua di Gio by Giorgio Armani is a Aromatic Aquatic fragrance for men. Acqua di Gio was launched in 1996.


Another amazing choice for a summer scent but compared to Platinum Egoiste, this one has a better persistance making it a good fragrance to wear for events or as a evening wear.

L'interdit givechy

L'Interdit Eau de Parfum by Givenchy is a Amber Floral fragrance for women. L'Interdit Eau de Parfum was launched in 2018.


For a women fragrance, honestly I do not think it can get better than this. The sublte balance between the fruity notes(Pear and Bergamot) and floral notes (Tuberose and Jasmines) is absolutely beautiful, and on top of that the fragrance also has a pretty wild side of Patchouli, Vanilla and Ambroxan making it a great choice for any occasions.
